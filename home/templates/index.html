How Does It Work From Here?

How to get from idea to finished product...

  • Register an account

    To gain access to the client portal you must first login. If you are a new user, you will need to register an account

  • Browse through services

    Browse through the services available by navigating to the services page and pick the service you require.

  • Customize your project

    Adjust the options on the form to tailor the service to your needs. Once you are happy press submit.

  • Your Quotation

    Depending on the service you have selected you will get a summary of the project details with an outlined cost. Please take this time to eagle eye all the details as this serves as my design guidelines and is the contractual agreement between you and me of the work being undertaken.

  • Like it or nah?

    Once you are 100% happy with the project details and costings, press the 'Give me the greenlight' button and you will be directed to pay the agreed project costs. Once this is done, I will start work as soon as possible and to the deadline agreed on the project details. You will be informed in good notice if there any changes and what consequences their is, if any.

  • Like it or nah?

    Well, I hope it's the former! But seriously my work thrives from the feedback you can give. So once you have your project delivered, I would really like it if you could give me a review and say how much you liked the service(fingers crossed!). Lastly but not least, I would love it if you would allow me to host your project on my online portfolio. Which will aid me in securing more fantastic clients like yourself.
